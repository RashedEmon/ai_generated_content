Exploring Dhaka: A Guide to the City of Mosques

Dhaka, the capital of Bangladesh, is a bustling city of over 18 million people. It is known as the City of Mosques for its many beautiful mosques, and it is also home to a vibrant culture, delicious food, and plenty of activities to keep visitors entertained. Whether you’re looking for a relaxing getaway or an exciting adventure, Dhaka has something for everyone.

Popular Places to Visit

  • Lalbagh Fort: Built in 1678, this Mughal fort is a popular tourist destination. It features a museum, mosque, and palace, as well as stunning views of the city.
  • Ahsan Manzil: This 19th-century palace was once the home of the Nawab of Dhaka. It is now a museum, showcasing the city’s history and culture.
  • National Parliament House: This iconic building is the seat of the Bangladeshi government. It is open to visitors, who can explore the grounds and take in the impressive architecture.
  • Bangladesh National Museum: This museum houses a large collection of artifacts from the country’s history, including sculptures, paintings, and coins.

Popular Food Items

  • Biriyani: A fragrant rice dish cooked with spices, meat, and vegetables.
  • Fuchka: A popular street food consisting of deep-fried dough balls filled with potatoes, chickpeas, and spices.
  • Kacchi: A type of curry made with raw marinated meat, potatoes, and spices.
  • Shorbot: A refreshing drink made with yogurt, sugar, and spices.

Activities to Do

  • River Cruises: Enjoy a relaxing cruise down the Buriganga River, with prices starting at $10 per person.
  • Cycling Tours: Explore the city on two wheels, with tours starting at $20 per person.
  • Cooking Classes: Learn how to make traditional Bangladeshi dishes, with classes starting at $30 per person.
  • Cultural Tours: Get an in-depth look at the city’s culture, with tours starting at $40 per person.

Dhaka is a vibrant city full of culture, history, and delicious food. From exploring the city’s many mosques to taking a river cruise, there is something for everyone in Dhaka. Whether you’re looking for a relaxing getaway or an exciting adventure, Dhaka is the perfect destination.
